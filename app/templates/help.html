How to Use 'What Key is This?'

Your guide to detecting musical keys from audio files.

The main function of this application is to detect the musical key of an audio file you provide and show the notes and chords of that scale with music score.

Steps to Get Your Results

  1. Inside the "Key listener" page you can find this in the main navigation menu.
  2. Upload your audio file. You have two options:
    • Drag and Drop: Simply drag your audio file directly onto the designated upload area.
    • Click to Upload: Click on the upload area to open your device's file explorer and select the audio file.
  3. Wait for the analysis. Once uploaded, a loading indicator will appear as the system analyzes the file.
  4. View the results. The application will display the detected key when the analysis is complete.
  5. If you click on "Show scale", it will load the page for that tonality's musical scale, chords, and relative key.

File Specifications

  • Supported Formats: MP3, WAV, and OGG.
  • Maximum Size: Up to 10MB.

Other Sections

The Scales and Chords pages are designed to be used independently of the key detection tool. They provide a comprehensive database for you to explore scales and chords in any key you choose.
